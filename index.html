Affordable Fun in Manchester

About Us

“All good adventures begin with a little curiosity.” – Alice in Wonderland

Curious Mancunian is a searchable repository, focused on providing discounted activities to students. Curious Mancunian’s origins started in 2023 with a requirement to creating ‘A Guide to Affordable Fun in Manchester’. The brainchild of four students that wanted to easily find cost saving things to see and do in Manchester.

Why Manchester? Why Not…as Ian Brown says, “Manchester’s got everything except a beach.”

Activities are becoming more and more expensive. We wanted a place to accommodate all interests with information on cost and location. Developed with students and young people in mind that are wanting their money to go a little bit further but not miss out on finding new interests or creating new connections. Curious Mancunian hopes to have a little something for everyone. We hope you are able to find what you are looking for or are inspired to try something new.
