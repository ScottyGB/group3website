Live Music

Manchester is home to some of the greatest musicians in the world such as The Smiths, Oasis, Joy Division and many more. From this many live music venues have popped up over the years hosting all the genres you could imagine. From intimate clubs and pubs to grand concert halls and arenas, Manchester offers a stage for both established artists and up-and-coming bands to showcase their talents. Iconic venues like the O2 Apollo, Albert Hall, and Gorilla have hosted legendary performances, while smaller venues like Night & Day Cafe and Soup Kitchen provide a more intimate setting for discovering new sounds.
