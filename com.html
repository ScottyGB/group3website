Comedy

Manchester has a rich history when it comes to comedy. The city has produced some of the UK's most beloved comedians, including Les Dawson, Victoria Wood, and Steve Coogan. In the 1980s and 1990s, the comedy scene in Manchester thrived, with many now-famous comedians getting their start in small venues such as The Comedy Store and The Frog and Bucket. Manchester's comedy clubs continue to attract both established and up-and-coming comedians, as well as audiences looking for a night of laughter. The city also hosts several annual comedy festivals, such as the Manchester Comedy Festival and the Women in Comedy Festival, which showcase a diverse range of performers and styles.
