Raves

The rave scene in Manchester started to emerge in the late 1980s and has thrived ever since! Many of Manchester’s iconic venues are held in abandoned warehouses and outdoor spaces that have been transformed into the perfect dancefloors. Manchester showcases many different genres from acid house to techno, and drum and bass. It’s a place where people can come together, let go and, dance all night long!
